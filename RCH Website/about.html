Who Are We?

We are a group of Intelligent high school students working to provide a safe space with support for new parents.
Being a parent can be really confusing and difficult, and Maternine aims to aid these individuals through easy access to the information that they need.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a website where any new parents can easily find articles that give them the information they need to accel in everyday life.
Our website should create a world where the confusion and distress that comes with being a new parent becomes eliminated.
